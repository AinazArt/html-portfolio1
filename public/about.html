About Me

Welcome! I'm a passionate artist with a diverse skill set in both traditional and digital mediums. with a background in architecture and years of experience in fine art, I bring a unique blend of precision and creativity to every project.

What I offer

  • Mixed media art:

    • I specialize in creating vibrant and detailed portraits using colored pencils, pastels, and ink. Each piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, capturing the essence and personality of the subject.

  • Digital illustration and portraits:

    • Whether you're looking for a modern, stylized digital portrait or custom illustration, I can bring your vision to life with high-quality digital artwork.

  • Architecture sketching:

    • Drawing on my architectural background, I create intricate and realistic sketching of buildings and structures, perfect for personal or professional use.

  • Pet portraits in pointillism:

    • I offer unique ink portraits of pets using the pointillism technique, a style that emphasizes texture and depth through the careful placement of individual dots.

I am committed to delivering artwork that not only meets but exceeds your expectations. Let's collaborate to create something truly special!
