The Best Movies According To Ainaz

My top 3 movies of all time

1+1, The Untouchables

This is my favourite movie. I like the French version. This story about aristocrat, who becomes a quadriplegic from a paragliding accident. and he hires young man from the projects to be his caregiver. And their lifes change. They help each other and become friends.

Harry Potter

Famous movies about young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends, all of whom attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardy.

Lord of The Rings

A story about Hobbit from the shire and eight companions who set out on a journey to destroy the powerful One Ring and save Middle-earth from the Dark Lord Sauron
